Founder & CEO
Farah Barnes
Architect · Lighting Designer · Washington DC

With over 30 years of experience in architecture and museum exhibition lighting, Farah Barnes founded IllumiArts to bridge fine art and consumer technology. Influenced by Bauhaus, Louis Kahn, Kandinsky and Andy Warhol, she holds degrees in Architecture from Virginia Tech and an MBA from George Washington University.

Her early career designing exhibitions at the National Gallery of Art sparked the vision for IA Smart — museum-quality illumination for creators everywhere.

  • Architecture degrees — Virginia Tech + GWU MBA
  • Founded APV Inc. — award-winning lighting consultancy (1994)
  • 25+ years museum & architectural lighting, national + international
  • National Gallery of Art exhibition lighting designer
