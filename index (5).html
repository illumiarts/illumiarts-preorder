Founder & CEO
Farah Barnes
Architect · Lighting Designer · Washington DC

With over 30 years in architecture and museum exhibition lighting, Farah Barnes founded IllumiArts to bridge fine art and consumer technology. Influenced by Bauhaus, Kandinsky, and Andy Warhol — she holds degrees in Architecture from Virginia Tech and an MBA from George Washington University.

Her career designing light exhibitions at the National Gallery of Art sparked the vision for IA Smart — museum-quality illumination for everyday creators.

  • Architecture — Virginia Tech + MBA from GWU
  • Founded APV Inc. — award-winning lighting consultancy (1994)
  • 25+ years museum & architectural lighting internationally
  • National Gallery of Art exhibition lighting designer
